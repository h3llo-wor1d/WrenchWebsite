Commonly Asked Project Muse Questions
Q: What Is Project Muse?
A: Project Muse is a unique live show in which VTubers are randomly picked, interviewed, and made custom BGM for live, on my stream, using a unique Discord bot I programmed.

Q: When are these streams?
A: Every Tuesday from 3:30PM EST to 9:30PM EST.

Q: Where Is Sign Up?
A: A link to the Discord is posted at 10:00PM EST the day prior to the stream.

Q: How does the application process work?
A: After this link is posted, registrants are able to fill out a form in the server using a new Discord feature called modals, which allow for in-app form popups.

Q: Who is eligible?
A: Anyone who is registered and NOT a bigot or terrible person is allowed, as I have no control over who the bot picks :)
Music Commission Information
Q: Do you take commissions?
A: I do, in fact, take commissions!

Q: How much do you charge per commission?
A: The price differs per commission based on genre, complexity, etc. I will usually estimate how much I think the amount of work is worth within a few hours of receiving them, but it usually is between 100-300.

Q: Where can I commission you?
A: You can commission me by inquiring either my Twitter or Discord DMs at any time! I will respond as soon as I can :)
